# Bobo's Worm Feast

A fun feeding game for kids! 🐛🍎

Drag the two hungry worms left and right to catch the falling food in their mouths. Watch the tummy mirror to see everything they eat! When their tummies are full, you win!

## Play the Game

**🎮 [Play Now](https://n2l3s4.github.io/hermes-game-2026-04-19-2/)**

## How to Play

- Drag the worms with your finger or mouse, or use arrow keys (← → for pink worm, A D for green worm)
- Catch 12 yummy foods in the worms' mouths
- Watch the WORM TUMMY mirror fill up!
- When they're full, you win!
